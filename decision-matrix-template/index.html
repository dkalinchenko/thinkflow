Decision Matrix Template FAQ

Common questions about using templates

Can I modify the criteria and weights in templates?

Absolutely. Templates are starting points. Once loaded, you can add, remove, or rename any criteria. Weights are fully adjustable too—just make sure they total 100%. The template gives you a head start; you customize it for your specific situation.

Do you have templates for decisions other than products?

Yes! While our product comparison templates are most popular, the same methodology works for any decision. Use our custom template builder for career decisions, vendor selection, location choices, or any scenario with multiple options and criteria.

Can I download templates for Excel or Google Sheets?

Yes. After creating or customizing a decision matrix in our tool, use the export function to download as CSV. This opens directly in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet app. You can also export as PDF for presentations.

What's the difference between using a template online vs. spreadsheet?

Our online tool offers AI-powered suggestions, automatic score calculation, visual charts, and one-click sharing. Spreadsheets require manual formula setup but offer unlimited customization. Most users prefer the convenience of our online tool.

How were the default weights determined?

Default weights are based on consumer research and expert opinions about what factors most influence satisfaction in each category. For example, camera users consistently rate image quality as most important, so it gets the highest weight. You should adjust based on YOUR priorities.
