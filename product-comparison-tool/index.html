Product Comparison Tool FAQ

Questions about comparing products online

How is this different from CNET, Wirecutter, or other review sites?

Review sites tell you what THEY think is best based on their criteria. Our product comparison tool lets YOU define the criteria and weights. A reviewer might prioritize camera quality, but if you care more about battery life and price, their "best phone" isn't best for you. We give you the framework to find YOUR best choice.

Can I compare products from different brands?

Absolutely. Our tool is brand-agnostic. Compare Apple to Samsung, Sony to Bose, or any combination. The weighted scoring methodology works regardless of brand, helping you focus on features and performance rather than marketing.

Where does the product data come from?

When you use our AI research feature, we pull product information including specs, features, prices, and ratings from Amazon. This gives you access to comprehensive product data without hours of manual research. You can also enter your own data manually.

Is this free to use?

Yes, completely free with no limitations. No trial period, no premium tier, no hidden fees. We believe everyone deserves access to objective decision-making tools. The tool runs in your browser with no server costs on our end.

Can I save and return to my comparison later?

Yes. Your comparisons are automatically saved in your browser's local storage. You can also export as a shareable link to access from any device, or download as PDF/CSV for permanent records.
