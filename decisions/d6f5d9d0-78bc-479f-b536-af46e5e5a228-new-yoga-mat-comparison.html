Methodology

The products were evaluated using a weighted scoring system across six criteria deemed essential for yoga mat selection. Each criterion was assigned a weight reflecting its relative importance to the average practitioner (e.g., Grip & Traction at 2.0 being most critical). Individual scores (1-5) were assigned based on performance, material specs, and user feedback, then multiplied by their respective weights to calculate a total score. This quantitative approach provides an objective comparison, highlighting which mats excel in areas that matter most.
