New Vacuum Cleaner Comparison

Published on January 24, 2026

Compare vacuums for your cleaning needs

Choosing the right vacuum cleaner can transform a tedious chore into an efficient task, directly impacting the cleanliness and health of a home. The decision balances raw cleaning power against convenience, versatility against budget, and immediate needs against long-term value. After a detailed evaluation of four leading models across six weighted criteria—Suction Power, Filtration, Versatility, Battery Life, Weight & Maneuverability, and Value for Money—the Dyson V15 Detect Cordless Vacuum Cleaner emerges as the top performer. It secured a weighted score of 38 points (39%), excelling in the highest-weighted categories. However, its premium price presents a significant trade-off, making alternatives like the Shark Vertex Pro and the budget-friendly Bissell CleanView Swivel Pet compelling choices for different user priorities.

The evaluation was conducted using a weighted scoring model to objectively compare each vacuum cleaner. Each of the six criteria was assigned a weight based on its relative importance to the overall decision for a general user. Scores (out of 5) for each vacuum were then multiplied by these weights and summed to produce a total weighted score, allowing for a balanced comparison that goes beyond simple price or feature lists.

Understanding the Evaluation Criteria: What Makes a Great Vacuum?

To make an informed choice, it's crucial to understand what each criterion measures and why it matters for daily use.

  • Suction Power (Weight: 2.0): This is the engine of cleaning performance. High suction power is essential for removing embedded debris from carpets, fine dust from hard floors, and stubborn pet hair. It's the foundation of a vacuum's effectiveness across all surfaces.
  • Filtration (Weight: 1.7): For allergy sufferers or pet owners, filtration is critical. A vacuum with a high-quality, sealed HEPA filtration system captures and traps microscopic allergens, dust mites, and dander rather than blowing them back into the air.
  • Versatility (Weight: 1.6): A versatile vacuum adapts to different cleaning challenges. This includes having the right attachments for upholstery, crevices, and pet hair, as well as the ability to transition seamlessly from thick carpets to delicate hardwoods without losing effectiveness.
  • Battery Life (Weight: 1.5): For cordless models, runtime dictates cleaning freedom. Sufficient battery life ensures the user can clean an entire floor or home without interruption. Battery performance on maximum suction mode is a key differentiator.
  • Weight & Maneuverability (Weight: 1.4): Ease of use is paramount. A lightweight, well-balanced vacuum reduces fatigue, makes cleaning stairs less daunting, and allows for easy maneuvering around and under furniture.
  • Value for Money (Weight: 1.5): This criterion evaluates the overall package relative to the price. It considers whether the performance, features, and build quality justify the investment, identifying which models deliver the most for the money.

Head-to-Head Vacuum Cleaner Analysis

Dyson V15 Detect Cordless Vacuum Cleaner

The Dyson V15 Detect represents the peak of cordless stick vacuum technology, engineered for deep, intelligent cleaning. Its standout feature is a green laser diode on the cleaner head that illuminates microscopic dust particles invisible to the naked eye, ensuring no debris is missed.

  • Performance Strengths: It earned a perfect 5/5 in Suction Power, driven by a Hyperdymium motor that delivers exceptional performance on all surfaces. Its Filtration also scored 5/5, with a fully sealed HEPA system that makes it an outstanding choice for allergy sufferers. The vacuum intelligently adjusts suction based on dust levels, optimizing power and battery use.
  • Considerations: The primary trade-off for this top-tier performance is a Value for Money score of just 2/5, reflecting its premium $749 price tag. Furthermore, while versatile (4/5), its Battery Life is a limitation, scoring 3/5 as runtime drops to 20-30 minutes on max power, which may not suffice for larger homes without a mid-clean recharge.

Shark Vertex Pro Cordless Vacuum with DuoClean PowerFins

The Shark Vertex Pro is a formidable competitor, offering high-end features at a more accessible price point. It focuses on practical innovation, such as a self-cleaning brushroll that actively removes hair wrap and LED headlights on the nozzle to reveal dust in dark spaces.

  • Performance Strengths: It matches the Dyson with a 5/5 in Suction Power, thanks to its effective DuoClean PowerFins technology. It also offers excellent Versatility (4/5) for pet hair and multi-surface cleaning and is relatively easy to handle, scoring 4/5 for Weight & Maneuverability.
  • Considerations: The Filtration system, while good, is not as robust as the Dyson's sealed HEPA design, earning a 3/5. Its most significant limitation is Battery Life (2/5), with about 30 minutes of runtime on high power. At $449, its Value for Money score is a middle-of-the-road 3/5, as the battery constraint is notable at this price.

iRobot Roomba j7+ Self-Emptying Robot Vacuum

The iRobot Roomba j7+ represents a different philosophy: automation over manual effort. It is a "set it and forget it" solution that uses PrecisionVision Navigation to avoid common obstacles like cords and pet waste, and it empties itself into a base that holds 60 days of dirt.

  • Performance Strengths: Its defining strength is in Weight & Maneuverability, scoring a perfect 5/5 for its fully autonomous operation. It also has strong Battery Life (4/5) with smart recharge-and-resume technology and good Filtration (4/5) for allergens.
  • Considerations: By design, it lacks Versatility (2/5), as it cannot clean stairs, upholstery, or above-floor surfaces. Its Suction Power (3/5) is sufficient for maintenance on hard floors and low-pile carpets but cannot match the deep cleaning of an upright or stick vacuum, especially on thick carpets. At $799, its high Value for Money depends entirely on how much the user prioritizes convenience over comprehensive cleaning capability.

Bissell CleanView Swivel Pet Upright Vacuum

The Bissell CleanView is a purpose-built, corded workhorse designed for pet owners on a budget. It eschews high-tech features for reliable, powerful suction and specialized tools aimed at tackling pet hair and messes.

  • Performance Strengths: It delivers outstanding Value for Money (4/5), providing capable cleaning for just $169. It is also highly Versatile for pet owners (4/5), coming with dedicated tools for hair removal and performing well on various floor types.
  • Considerations: As a corded upright, it scores lowest in Battery Life (1/5), as it is tethered to an outlet. Its Filtration (2/5) lacks a true HEPA filter, making it less ideal for severe allergies. While its swivel steering helps, its Weight & Maneuverability (3/5) is hampered by a bulky design that makes stair cleaning cumbersome.

Verdict and Final Recommendations

The Dyson V15 Detect is the recommended choice for users who want the best overall cleaning performance and have the budget to support it. Its victory is anchored by top scores in the two most important criteria: Suction Power and Filtration. The laser dust detection is a genuine innovation that guarantees a deeper clean. However, this premium performance commands a premium price, and the battery life requires mindful management.

The right vacuum ultimately depends on specific needs and constraints:

  • Choose the Dyson V15 Detect if: Budget is secondary to obtaining the highest-performance cordless vacuum with best-in-class filtration and innovative cleaning technology for a whole-home, deep clean.
  • Choose the Shark Vertex Pro if: You want powerful cordless performance and smart features like a self-cleaning brushroll but seek a better value proposition than the Dyson, accepting shorter battery life as a trade-off.
  • Choose the iRobot Roomba j7+ if: Daily, hands-free floor maintenance is the top priority. It’s ideal for supplementing a manual vacuum or for those who prefer automation, provided that deep carpet cleaning and above-floor versatility are not required.
  • Choose the Bissell CleanView Swivel Pet if: You are a pet owner on a strict budget who needs reliable, effective cleaning power and specialized tools, and you do not mind the limitations of a corded, bulkier upright model.
