Key Criteria for Phone Comparison

When comparing smartphones, focus on these factors:

  • Camera system: Megapixels matter less than sensor size and processing
  • Battery life: Real-world tests, not just mAh capacity
  • Performance: SoC benchmarks and daily smoothness
  • Display: Resolution, refresh rate, brightness, panel type
  • Software: Update duration, bloatware, features
  • Value: What you get relative to price
