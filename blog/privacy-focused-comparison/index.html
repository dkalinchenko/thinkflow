Why Choose Privacy-Focused Comparison Tools?

When you research products online, you're giving away valuable data about your interests, purchasing intent, and preferences. Privacy-focused comparison tools let you shop and compare without becoming the product yourself.

What Happens to Your Shopping Data

Most comparison sites and shopping tools:

  • Track every product you view and compare
  • Share this data with advertising networks
  • Sell your "shopping intent" signals to data brokers
  • Enable retargeting ads that follow you across the web
  • May contribute to dynamic pricing (different prices for different users)

Benefits of Privacy-Focused Tools

  • No targeted ads: Your research doesn't follow you around the internet
  • Fair pricing: Avoid potential dynamic pricing based on your purchase intent
  • Data ownership: Your comparison data stays on your device
  • No profiling: Shopping habits aren't added to your digital profile
  • Peace of mind: Research freely without surveillance
